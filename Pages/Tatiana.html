PhD Student

Received the B.S. degrees in industrial engineering and computer science from Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia in 2016. In August 2016, she started her Ph.D. degree in Electronic Engineering under the supervision of professor Henry Arguello at Universidad Industrial de Santander. Her research interests include compressive spectral imaging, low-rank approximation and numerical optimization.
