PhD Student

Received her B.Sc. in Systems Engineering in 2015, and her M.Sc. degree in Systems and Computer Engineering in 2017, both from the Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia. She is currently a PhD student in the Computer Engineering Department at the Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia. Her research interests include compressive spectral imaging, computational imaging, high dimensional signal processing and compressive sensing.
