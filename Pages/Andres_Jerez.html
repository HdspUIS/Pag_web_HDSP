Master Student

Andrés Felipe Jerez is a master student of applied mathematics of the Department of Physics at the Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia. He received the bachelor of science degree in Computer Science from Universidad Industrial de Santander in 2017. His research interest includes image processing, compressive computed tomography, phase retrieval, and hyperspectral imaging.
