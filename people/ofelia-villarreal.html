Ofelia Patricia Villarreal Dulcey

PhD Student

Graduated as Bachelor of Electronic Engineering in 2006 at Universidad del Valle, Colombia; as Master of Sciences in Electronic Engineering in 2014 at the Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia and as Master in Electrical & Computer Engineering in 2014 at the University of Delaware, DE, USA. Her main research areas are image processing, compressive sensing and remote sensing.
