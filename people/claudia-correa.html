Claudia Victoria Correa Pugliese

Postdoctoral Researcher

Received the B.S. and M.S. in computer science from Universidad Industrial de Santander, Bucaramanga, Colombia, in 2009 and 2013, respectively, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical and computer engineering from University of Delaware, Newark, DE, in 2013 and 2017, respectively. Her research interests include compressive spectral imaging, computational imaging, optical coding and compressed sensing.
